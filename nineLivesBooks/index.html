Nine Lives books

Nine Lives Books is a premier used book store located in San Antonio, Texas. We specialize in used merchandise, but we have some new merchandise, too. We have books, records, DVDs, videotapes, music CDs, music audiotapes, books on CD, books on audiotape, magazines, comics, nick nacks, healing stones, and more.

Diverse Inventory

9 Lives Books has a very diverse inventory. We have everything from childrens books, to mystery books, to thrillers. We also carry a large assortment of comics, stickers, bookmarks buttons and even more fun nicknacks.

Books

We have a huge inventory of books here. We have hardback books, softbacks, leather covered books,Audio books, Toddler board books and More!!

Comic Books

Our huge customer base, loves our comic book section. We get guy's coming in here every day telling us how much they love the huge selection of comic books that we have available. So Stop by and check them out!
